About The Story

"The Carver of Names" follows Zayan, a young orphan and apprentice calligrapher in the city of Al-Miraj. Tasked with carving the 99 Names of Allah on wooden plaques day after day, he finds the work repetitive and yearns to create something that tells his own story.

When a prestigious art competition is announced, Zayan sees his chance for recognition and creates a magnificent carving of a legendary hero. But the wise Qadi's response shatters his ambitions, sending him on a downward spiral of failure and self-doubt.

Through this crisis, Zayan begins a profound personal journey with the Names he once carved mechanically. As he carves each Name with his newfound questions and struggles, he discovers their living meanings and transforms from a skilled craftsman into a spiritual seeker.

This 3,000+ word Islamic story for youth explores themes of humility, divine love, patience through trial, and finding one's true purpose through connection with the Divine Attributes.
